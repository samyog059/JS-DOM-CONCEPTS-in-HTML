To My Forever

Seven little chapters of us

If love had a louder word, I would use it for you. You turn ordinary seconds into a constellation of bright, soft, wild moments. A rose morning, a brave proposal, chocolate-midnight secrets, a teddy-soft hush, wrap-tight hugs, slow-burning kisses, and the day that feels written just for us — every piece of this week hums with you. Thank you for being both the calm and the spark — my favorite plot twist, my truest home, the story I choose in every season.

Always yours, Samyog Pangeni

P.S. No matter the day — rose, propose, chocolate, teddy, hugs, kisses, or the finale — every path still circles back to your heart.
